About This Accommodation

Yellow House is dedicated to supporting NSFAS-funded students at DUT Campus. The property provides comfortable and cozy accommodation specifically designed for students who depend on NSFAS funding for their housing.

With flexible payment options and supportive management, Yellow House makes it easier for NSFAS students to secure quality housing. Manager Mvelo Ruffus is committed to ensuring all residents feel welcome and supported.
